I went through a lot of disappointments, but what matter the most was the support and unity of my family. It was hard to see all my classmates apply for colleges and scholarships; therefore Base Camp has been the greatest thing that has happened to me. Base Camp is not just a program in my opinion is like,

An open door to be someone in life and we can either decide whether to step in or be left outside.
